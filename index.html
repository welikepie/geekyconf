What

GeekyConf is a day celebrating geekiness in all its forms, packed with talks aimed to inspire and entertain. Learn about peoples' passions' and learn about different areas of geeky science, art and culture.

Where & When

Nov 21

As the winter months draw in we hope the passion of GeekyConf will keep you warm and keep your creativity brimming. On the 21st of November Geeks from many walks of life will descend on the eclectic venue that is Oval Space.

29-32 The Oval, London, E2 9DT

Something Extra

Throughout the day we'll be hosting an array of activities to make the day that little bit more memorable; Circut bending classes in our mini workshop and story readings with some lovely geeks including Jake Archibald and James Wallis and many more. We've invited Giddy Up Coffee to keep you all refreshed with the finest coffee and are making plans for tasty lunch. After all the talks have finished we will be making our way to London Fields Brewery.
