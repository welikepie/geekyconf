What is GeekyScience?

GeekyScience is an exciting evening of learning and entertainment. Each month, experts in their field will come together to share what they know, related to an overarching scientific theme that will change each month. GeekyScience is curated and hosted by Helen Arney (@helenarney), award-winning comedian and physics graduate.

September: Evolution

From Gregor Mendel's discovery of inheritance to Charles Darwin's theory of natural selection, the theory of evolution has led to an immense amount of scientific research and discovery.

Dr. Kat Arney (@harpistkat), Science Information Manager at Cancer Research UK, will be joining us to share her insights into the evolution of Cancer and Dr. Adam Rutherford (@adamrutherford), journalist and presenter, will be using hip hop in his exploration of evolution and synthetic biology.
