What is GeekyScience?

GeekyScience is an exciting evening of learning and entertainment. Each month, experts in their field will come together to share what they know, related to an overarching scientific theme that will change each month. GeekyScience is curated and hosted by Helen Arney (@helenarney), award-winning comedian and physics graduate.

October 31st: Spaaace

"Space is big. You just won't believe how vastly, hugely, mind- bogglingly big it is. I mean, you may think it's a long way down the road to the chemist's, but that's just peanuts to space." - Douglas Adams, The Hitchhikers Guide to the Galaxy

Our third edition of GeekyScience is all about SPACE! Sheila Kenani will be doing a whistle stop tour of the solar system, Colin Stuart will be teaching us all about black holes and Lewis Dartnell will be informing us of what an Alien might look like. There will be asteroids! And more to be announced.
